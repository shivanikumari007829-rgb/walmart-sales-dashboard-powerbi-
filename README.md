#  Walmart Sales Analytics Dashboard

##  Overview

This project is an interactive Walmart Sales Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer ratings, payment methods, and city-wise business performance.

The goal of this project is to transform raw retail data into meaningful visual insights that can support business decision-making.


##  Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Track monthly sales and profit trends.
* Identify top and bottom profit-generating cities.
* Understand customer payment preferences.
* Compare sales across product categories.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings.


##  Tools Used

* Power BI

* Data Visualization

##  Dashboard Features

### Monthly Sales & Profit Analysis

Tracks sales and profit performance across months to identify trends and seasonal patterns.

### Payment Method Distribution

Analyzes customer preferences among:

* Credit Card
* E-Wallet
* Cash

### Customer Rating Analysis

Compares average ratings across product categories to measure customer satisfaction.

### Category-wise Sales Performance

Shows which product categories contribute the most to overall sales.

### Top 5 Profit-Making Cities

Identifies cities generating the highest profits.

### Bottom 5 Profit-Making Cities

Highlights locations with the lowest profit contribution.

### Year Filter

Allows users to analyze performance across different years.


##  Key Insights

* Walmart generated over **1.21 Million** in total sales.
* Total profit exceeded **476 Thousand**.
* Credit Card was the most frequently used payment method.
* Food & Beverage received the highest average customer rating.
* Weslaco emerged as the most profitable city.
* Fashion Accessories and Home & Lifestyle categories contributed significantly to sales.
